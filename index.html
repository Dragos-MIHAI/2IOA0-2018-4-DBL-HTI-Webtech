Welcome to the Visualization Website of the Group 9. In this website you will have the opportunity to explore and analyze network data in a better way. Two views will be shown: one for a node-link diagram and one for an adjacency matrix. Both views are linked interactively, i.e., if someone changes one view, the other one should adapt to this change accordingly. From an algorithmic perspective, the node-link diagram is supporting at least 3 different advanced layout approaches (radial, force-directed, hierarchical) while the adjacency matrix is supporting at least 5 different matrix reordering techniques (Behrisch et al.). Moreover, further interaction techniques should be integrated following the survey by Yi et al.
